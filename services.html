Our Comprehensive Services

Discover our range of professional services designed to help your business thrive in today's competitive landscape.

Strategic Communications

NPDV provides tailored strategic communications solutions using the appropriate medium to the target audience while maintaining company goals using stakeholder feedback as a tool.

Training and Capacity Building

Client-centric on-site or off-site training and capacity building with expertise in an array of areas.

Career Coaching

We provide access to career resources, approaches and initiatives for clients to advance in their professions, whether vocational, artisanal or academic.

Project Management

The methodology we adopt focuses on the variables throughout the stages of project planning, development, commissioning and maintenance.

IT Solutions

NPDV will provide clients and organizations with information technology services that include but are not limited to advance technical support services, cloud services, IT procurement services, and Hardware fulfilment services.

Financial Advisory Services

We provide financial advisory services including debt management and asset recovery, risk management, investment and financial research analysis, and revenue enhancement and collection.

Risk Management Assessment

We provide comprehensive risk management and assessment services to help clients take a structured risk management approach at every stage of their project to identify, assess and manage risk related to project cost, performance, safety and timing.
